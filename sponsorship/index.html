Sponsorship Opportunities

The IEEE VizSec Symposium is inviting support from industry, academia, and government contributors in security and data visualization.

As a supporter, we encourage you to join us at VizSec 2016, colocated with VIS 2016 this October in Baltimore. Supporters receive several new benefits this year:

  • Recruitment — supporters will engage with the talented students and professionals that attend VizSec and VIS 2016, many of whom are actively seeking internships, employment, and collaboration opportunities.
  • Exhibition — many levels of support include dedicated exhibition space for your organization at VIS 2016.
  • Registration — most levels include one or more complimentary registrations for representatives from your organization.
  • Exposure — for all support levels, your organization’s logos will be prominently displayed at all conference events, on the conference web site, and in any conference materials.

Support Options

Please see the IEEE VIS Support Prospectus for support options. For more information on becoming a sponsor, reach out to Lane Harrison at lane@cs.wpi.edu.

Other Opportunities

We are open to other suggestions from supporters as well. Past attendees have offered several ideas for specific opportunities at VizSec organizations may be interested in supporting, including:

  • Diversity scholarships
  • Lunch, dinner, or meetups for attendees
  • Coffee breaks
  • Best Paper Award
